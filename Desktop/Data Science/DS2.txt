Why Data Science?
=====================
Data → raw facts, numbers, text, images, measurements, etc.
Science → a systematic way of studying something using observation, experiments, statistics, and reasoning.


Data Science means using data to extract useful information and make smart decisions.


1. Makes Big Data Useful
-------------------------
> Every day, Around 400–500 million terabytes (TB) of data created globally.
  ex:
	Like, Buy button, tweet, pull request, UPI and etc
 
Data science:
	> Cleans the data 
	 	1) Handling Missing Values (user left any field or data unavailable)
		2) Removing Duplicates
		3) Standardization(HYD) 
		4) Noise Removal (Typing mistakes)
	   	and etc.

	> Finds patterns
	  	1) Uses Algorithms ,Statistics, ML and Visualization to know what "story" the data is telling us.

	> Provides useful insights - Meaningful conclusion
	 	1) 
			Data shows: Sales increase during weekends.
			Conclusion: Increase stock and staff on weekends.		

		2) 	
			Analysis shows: Young users prefer online payments.
			Conclusion: Improve digital payment features.
	
		3) 
			Data reveals: Ads perform better at night.
			Conclusion: Run advertisements during evening hours.




2. Better Decision Making
--------------------------
With data science, companies don’t rely on guesswork instead they make decisions based on data.
Example: 
		> Which product is selling more and which is not.
		> Which course is in high demand and which is not.

		Later companies perform marketing or promoting the products and courses.
		
3. Solves Real-Life Problems
----------------------------
Data science is used for:

	> AWS Billing and Business Dashboards
          
	> Estimating Employee Cost 
	  (Forecast hiring needs or Layoff)

	> Finding Causes of Events (Behavior Analysis) : 
		1) Why customer purchased that particular product - He saw ads.
		2) People who buy Bread often also buy Butter - This helps recommend products.
		3) Why customer listened that song - Interested in.
	
	> Predicting population ,sales volume and dieseas in hospitals.
	  	1) Early we predict population based on sensors data or  birth and death rate
		2) Previous sales records, Customer behavior, Seasonal trends and Market demand
		3) Patient records,Medical history, Symptoms and Lab reports.

	> ODD or Suspicious Events
		1) Fraud Detection in banks

	> Managing traffic and building smart cities
	  	1) Data science helps control and optimize traffic flow using:
			1) GPS data
			2) Road sensors
			3) Traffic cameras
			4) IoT devices
		2) Air and Water Quality , smart energy management, public tranportation planning	


4. Foundation of AI and Machine Learning
-----------------------------------------
Without data science, AI and machine learning are not possible.
Examples:
	Netflix recommendations
	Amazon suggestions
	Google voice assistants



5. High-Demand Career
---------------------
Nowadays, data science is a high-demand field. It offers many job opportunities and good salaries.

Note:
-----
1) Collection the data : Python and Web Scraping.
