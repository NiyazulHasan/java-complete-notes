Probability	
===========
"Probability" or "Chance" is very commonly used in day to day conversation.
ex:
	RCB vs GT (who will win)


In statistical sense, the term "probability" is not connected with beliefs or any form of wishful-thinking.
It is one of the foundations of Data Science and Machine Learning. 
It is based on calculating numerical values.


Probability is a number which ranges from 0 to 1.
Zero for an event which cannot occur.
One for an event certain to occur.


Probability is a mathematical tool used to study randomness. It deals with the chance 
(the likelihood) of an even occuring.
ex:
	IPL Teams - 2026

	10 (10 teams) 
	---
	10 (10 changes)

				10
	Each team probability = --- = 1 
				10

	Each team probability is 1 or 10%. 



Probability formulea:
----------------------
			Number of Favourable Cases(a)
	p =  ---------------------------------------------
		Total Number of equally Likely Cases (n) 

	Here p + q = 1 
	p - Probability of occurance (Success)
	q - Probability of non-occurance (Failure)

Example1
--------
	Consider Toss in a match.
	ex:
		Head / Tail 

		     1	(Head)
		p = ----
	             2  (two options)


		
		     1	(Tails)
		q = ----
	             2  (two options)

		Here half probability is there for head and tail.

Example2
---------
	Consider rolling a fair dice:			
	
	ex:
		dice : 1,2,3,4,5,6 
		
		What is the probability for getting 4?
		ex:
			1  (one time)
		      ------
			6  (probability from 6)
					
			
		Hence p = 1/6 
			

		What is the probability for not getting 4?

		p + q = 1
		
		1
		-- + q = 1 
		6 
			  1		
		q =  1 - --- 
			  6
		q = 5/6


Example3
----------
	Consider rolling a fair dice:			
	
	ex:
		dice : 1,2,3,4,5,6 
		
		What is the probability for getting even numbers?
		ex:
			even = 2,4,6
			odd = 1,3,5
	
			Here even and odd we have two attributes.

			even number probability = 1/2 
			odd number probability = 1/2
