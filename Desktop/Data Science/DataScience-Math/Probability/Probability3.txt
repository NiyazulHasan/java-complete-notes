Poisson Distribution
====================
The Poisson distribution is a probability model used to describe how many times an event happens in a fixed interval (time, space, area, etc.) when those events occur randomly and independently at a constant average rate.

ex:
	How many customers arrive at a store in an hour?
	How many typing errors appear on a page?
	How many calls a call center gets per minute?
