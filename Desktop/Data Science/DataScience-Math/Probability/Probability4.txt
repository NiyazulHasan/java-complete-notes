Normal Distribution
===================
A normal distribution is one of the most important concepts in statistics. It describes how values of a variable are spread out in a symmetrical, bell-shaped curve.

1. Shape (Bell Curve)
---------------------
> Mean is located at center of the distribution.
> Symmetrical/Same from Mean Line.
> Two tails of the distribution extends indefinitely but never touches the x axis.
> Mean = Mediam = Mode (all are same in normal distribution)
> The percentage distribution of area under standard normal curve is broadly as follow
  ex:
	±1σ = 68.27%
	
	Here 1σ is one sigma
	Here σ is a standard deviation 

	±2σ = 95.44%
	
	Here 2σ is two sigma
