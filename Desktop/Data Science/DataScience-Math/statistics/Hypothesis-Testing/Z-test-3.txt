Question1
=========
In a random sample of 1000 persons from Delhi, 500 are found to be consumer of rice. In another random sample from 
Mumbai 650 out of 1200 are found to be consumers of rice. Do these data reveal a significant difference between these 
two cities so far as a proportion of rice consumers at 1% of level of significance?


p	Significance Level		Two Tailed Test 	One Tailed Test
---	-------------------		---------------		----------------
0.1	10%				1.65			1.28		
0.05    5%				1.95			1.64
0.01	1%				2.58			2.33
0.001	0.1%				3.29			3.10


Ho : p1  = p2
Ha : p1 != p2

Given Data 
		n1=1000  , x1=500
		n2=1200  , x2=650 
			 500
		 p1 =    ---- = 0.5
	                 1000
	
		 	650 
		 p2 =  ------ = 0.54   
			1200

		    1000*0.5 + 1200*0.54	500 + 650	1150
		p = --------------------- = 	---------  =    ----- = 0.52
			1000 + 1200		  2200	 	2200


		 q = 1 - 0.52 = 0.48

Hence there is no significance difference.
