Question1
==============

A coin was tossed 484 times and head turned up 265 times. Test the hypothesis that coin is unbiased?

p	Significance Level		Two Tailed Test 	One Tailed Test
---	-------------------		---------------		----------------
0.1	10%				1.65			1.28		
0.05    5%				1.95			1.64
0.01	1%				2.58			2.33
0.001	0.1%				3.29			3.10
	
Let consider coin is unbiase (Null Hypthesis)

H0 : No of heads = No of Tails 
Ha : No of heads != no of Tails

Given Data : n=484, x=265, p = 0.5 q = 0.5


Note:
----
An unbiased coin means:

Probability of Head = Probability of Tail
ex:
	p(Head)=p(Tail)
	So:
		p=0.5 ,q = 1−p = 0.5


Formulea 

               x - np
       z =    --------
	 	_____
            	V npq
​​
ex:
	        265 - 484*0.5       265 - 242      23
       z =    --------------   =   ---------   =  ---  = 2.09
	 	____________	     ________       11
            	V   484*0.5*0.5	    V	121


Note:
-----
	Zcal = 2.09  and Ztab = 1.96	

	Here H0 is reject.
	Coin is biased



Question 2
===========
In 256 throws a six faced dice. Odd points appeared 122 times. Would you say that the dice is fair at 5% level of significance?


p	Significance Level		Two Tailed Test 	One Tailed Test
---	-------------------		---------------		----------------
0.1	10%				1.65			1.28		
0.05    5%				1.95			1.64
0.01	1%				2.58			2.33
0.001	0.1%				3.29			3.10

Lets consider dice is fair (Null Hypothesis)

H0 : No of odd number = No of even number 
Ha : No of odd number != No of even number 

Given Data :

		n=256, x=122, p=0.5, q=0.5

		Dice : 1,2,3,4,5,6
		odd  : 1,3,5 ( p = 1/2 (odd changes 1 out of 2))  -  0.5
		even : 2,4,6 ( 1 = 1/2 (even changes 1 out of 2)) -  0.5

Formulea 

               x - np
       z =    --------
	 	_____
            	V npq
​​
ex:
	        122 - 256*0.5       122 - 128      -6
       z =    --------------   =   ---------   =   ---  =  -0.75
	 	____________	     ________       8 
            	V  256*0.5*0.5	    V	64

	Note: 
	----
		256*0.5  - half of 256 is 128
		256*0.5*0.5  - half of 256 is 128 , half of 128 is 64


		Zcal  = -0.75   , Ztab = 1.96  

		-1.96
			-0.75    (Here H0 is Accept)
		+1.96
