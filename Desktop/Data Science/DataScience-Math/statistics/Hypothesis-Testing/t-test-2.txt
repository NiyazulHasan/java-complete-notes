To Test the significance of the Mean of Random sample 
=====================================================
Diagram: t-test-2.1

Note:
-----
In a t-test, we usually do not know the population standard deviation (σ). Since σ is unknown, we estimate it using the sample standard deviation (s).
ex:
	If σ is known → Use a z-test
	If σ is unknown → Use a t-test	

	Here  σ is replace with 's' in formulea.
