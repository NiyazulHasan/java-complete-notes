Distance of a point from plane 
==============================
The distance from a point to a plane is the length of the shortest (perpendicular) line segment from the point to the plane.

Diagram : linear-algebra5.2


Circle
==================
A circle in 2D is the set of all points at a fixed distance r from a center (a,b).

Diagram: linear-algebra5.3


Sphere
=======
A sphere in 3D is the set of all points at a fixed distance r from a center (a,b,c).

Diagram: linear-algebra5.4


Ellipse 
=======
An ellipse is the set of all points in a plane whose distances from two fixed points (called foci) add up to a constant.

Diagram: linear-algebra5.5


Square Rectangle 
================
A square is a special type of rectangle in which 

All four angles are 90 degree.
